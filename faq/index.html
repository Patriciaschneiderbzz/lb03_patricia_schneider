What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The technique consists of five steps: formulate the task in writing. Set the short-time alarm clock for 25 minutes. work on the task until the alarm clock rings; mark with an X. take a short break (5 minutes). after every four pomodori take a longer break (15-20 minutes).

How does the Eisenhower Technique work?

The Eisenhower Principle is a time management method that distinguishes important and urgent tasks from unimportant and non-urgent tasks. The key here is that important tasks are completed immediately and unimportant tasks are either delegated or eliminated.
